Raiju

- When a Raiju manifests during a ghost event or a hunt, it has a larger range from which it can interfere with electronic equipment.

- When nearby activated electronic equipment, the Raiju's hunt sanity threshold is increased to 65%, similar to a Yokai with talking.

- If a Raiju is within 10 metres of at least one piece of electronic equipment, it will have an increased movement speed of approximately 2.5 m/s.
