Yurei

- During a ghost event or a hunt, if the player is within 10 metres of a Yurei, their sanity will drop by 0.4% per second compared to 0.2% per second with most other ghosts.

- A Yurei has the ability to drop the sanity of all players within 7.5 metres by about 12.5%. When this happens, one selected door in the room that the ghost is currently in will close, similar to a manifestation ghost event, but the ghost will not actually appear or hiss.

- The Yurei will also prefer ghost events where it does a "hiss".

- When Smudge Sticks are used on the Yurei, it will not leave the room that it is currently in for 90 seconds.
