Onryo

- Onryo have a hunt sanity threshold of 60%.

- When a lit candle, lighter, or campfire (in Maple Lodge Campsite) is extinguished near an Onryo, whether by itself or blown out by the ghost, there is a chance for the Onryo to begin a hunt, regardless of average sanity.

- If a flame is present near the ghost, the flame acts as a crucifix, preventing it from hunting within a range of 4m. If it attempts to start a hunt within range of the flame, the hunt will fail and it will blow out the candle instead.

- The Onryo also has a higher chance to blow out a flame than other ghosts, further complicating the task of countering one.
