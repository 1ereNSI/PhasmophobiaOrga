Hantu

- During a hunt, Hantu's speed is affected by the temperature of the rooms that it goes through:

Room temperature Ghost speed (m/s)
> 15°C 1.4
12°C 1.75
9°C 2,1
6°C 2,3
3°C 2.5
0°C 2.7

- The Hantu is twice as likely to choose turning off the breaker as an interaction and can't turn it on, compared to other ghosts..

- When the Hantu manifests itself during a hunt, it will emit freezing breath when it is in a room with a temperature equal to or lower than 3°C (37.4°F). Dead players cannot see this breath.

- On Nightmare difficulty, a Hantu will always have Freezing Temperatures as one of its two pieces of evidence.
