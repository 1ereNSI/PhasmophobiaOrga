Mare

- If a player turns on a light near a Mare, it has a chance of turning the light off almost immediately. The Mare also has a higher chance of wandering into darker rooms. Mares are more likely to choose turning off lights as an interaction.

- A Mare has a modified hunt sanity threshold depending on whether the room it is currently in is lit. If the lights are turned off, the hunt sanity threshold is increased 60%, and if the lights are turned on, the threshold is decreased to 40%.

- When performing a ghost event, Mares are more likely to choose one where the light bulbs explode.
