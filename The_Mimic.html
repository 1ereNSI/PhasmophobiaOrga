The Mimic

- The Mimic is able to mirror the traits of other ghosts. The Mimic may occasionally change the ghost type that it chooses to imitate every 30 seconds to about 2 minutes.

- When hunting, a Myling's footstep sounds will only be audible to players within 9 meters, similar to the distance required for electronic interference.

- The Mimic also provides "evidence" of Ghost Orbs in addition to its three main evidence types, which also appear on Nightmare difficulty.
