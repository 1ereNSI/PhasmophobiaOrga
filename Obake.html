Obake

- When interacting with a valid surface, the Obake has a 75% chance of creating fingerprints, instead of the usual 100%. Additionally, when creating handprints, there is a one-sixth chance (~16.6%) that it will create a unique handprint that has 6 fingers

- The Obake occassionally triggers an ability to reduce the remaining time for all existing, lingering fingerprints on the map by half.

- On Nightmare Difficulty, an Obake will always have Fingerprints evidence as one of its two evidence types.
