Oni

- The Oni is more active, interacting with objects more often and causing more ghost events.

- When interacting with objects, an Oni has a chance of throwing it higher and/or with more force, making it land further away.

- An Oni cannot perform the smoke/mist-type ghost event that causes a hiss, but can still perform a manifestation (ghost) event and may hiss in that case.

- It also has a higher chance of showing in its full form during a manifestation event as opposed to a shadowy or translucent form.
